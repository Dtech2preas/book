About DTECH Book Exchange

DTECH Book Exchange is a student-focused platform designed to make buying and selling textbooks easier. Instead of scrolling through endless group chats, students can view all available books in one organized place. Each listing includes the book details, price, and direct contact information, allowing buyers and sellers to connect quickly and conveniently. Our goal is to simplify the process and create a better experience for students.

DTECH is an online ecosystem run by an entity called PREASX24.
